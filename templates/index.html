Building collaborative works of literary art

Forking Stories is a place for budding storywriters to try their hand at creating a collaborative masterpiece.

Hosts submit a story beginning, and sets some rules to make the task more challenging. Collaborators add to the story, creating forks, (diverging paths) to whole new stories.

Not feeling inspired today ? Make your mark by voting for the best submissions submitted, and help build the story

forking

Verb: (esp. of a road or other route)

Divide into two parts: "the place where the road forks".
